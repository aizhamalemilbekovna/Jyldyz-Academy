About Us

Jyldyz Academy is a digital nomad community, our collective vision is to make 21st century learning a reality for everyone.

Our team of experienced educators, along with communication, technology and business professionals, innovates in a comprehensive soft skills learning process.

We are collaborating with all stakeholders to create the Portrait of the Graduate of the 21st Century.

We offer an educational experience that prepares all students to **lifelong learn** and contribute to an ever-changing world.

Our Goals

  • Promoting the "Portrait of a Graduate of the 21st Century" taking into account the global requirements of companies and the international labor market;
  • Teaching students "flexible skills" to implement leadership, a lifelong learning paradigm;
  • Promotion of the IT industry as a profession of the future or as a basis for the growth of independence and stability in the 21st century;
  • Increasing the knowledge of children / youth / adults about new forms of income generation in the family, improving skills, abilities, expanding economic rights and opportunities;
  • Uniting IT communities from different countries in order to consolidate and exchange experiences, ideas and solutions aimed at the sustainable development of the country's economic asset.
  • Reduction of poverty in regions, communities, families through an increase in income from new skills, projects, ideas, initiatives in the labor market;
  • Reducing the gap between our country and other states through the knowledge economy and its export;
  • Transformation of knowledge and orientation of "schoolchildren" to the challenges and requirements of the 21st century through a conscious choice;
